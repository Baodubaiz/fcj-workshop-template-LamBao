# SUMMARY REPORT: "FIRST CLOUD JOURNEY MEETUP"

## General Event Information

* **Event Name:** First Cloud Journey Meetup
* **Date & Time:** 09:00 - 12:00, May 30, 2026
* **Location:** 26th Floor, Bitexco Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City
* **Role:** Attendee

---

## Presentations & Main Highlights

### 1. GitHub Open Source Floci
* **Core Topic:** Introduction to the open-source Floci project on GitHub.
* **Details:** The speaker shared approach methods to help learners practice cloud labs/projects while minimizing AWS credit spending. Visually engaging games were also introduced to make learning and remembering AWS services easier and more interactive.

### 2. Sharing about Hackathons & Igniting the Passion
* **Speakers:** Co-presented by 3 young, highly experienced speakers.
* **Details:** Shared their journey in hackathons, including the initial obstacles and how they overcame them. This session aimed to motivate and inspire participants to build confidence and step forward to try out real-world hackathon competitions.

### 3. Collaboration & Teamwork when Freelancing
* **Details:** Focused on project coordination and management between multiple teams and members when freelancing, as well as sharing personal work experiences.

### 4. Overcoming Shyness & Lack of Confidence
* **Details:** A deep-dive analysis of shyness and self-doubt. The presenter highlighted how lack of confidence makes people lose valuable opportunities in both academic and professional development, encouraging attendees to step outside their comfort zones.

### 5. "Tu vi Dai Viet" Project - Bridging AI and Traditional Horoscope Consultation
* **Details:** Presented an innovative horoscope consultation website (tuvidaiviet.com) that integrates modern AI technology with professional horoscope consulting experience to deliver personalized user experiences. The speaker also discussed testing a change in the deployment workflow using AWS cloud services.

### 6. The Costs of Procrastination
* **Details:** The final talk addressed procrastination and delay habits. The speaker explained how procrastination impedes progress, dragging individuals behind and causing them to miss key advancement opportunities in both life and work.

---

## Key Takeaways & Action Plan

### New Knowledge & Mindsets:
* **Cost Optimization:** Discovered the Floci open-source tool, helping save budgets during AWS learning.
* **Proactive Mindset:** Realized the negative impacts of shyness and procrastination, learning to be more confident in identifying and seizing opportunities.
* **Practical Collaboration:** Gained insights into freelance team operations and workflow alignment.
* **Technology Innovation:** Witnessed the potential of combining AI with traditional fields (e.g., Tu vi Dai Viet) and deploying them on AWS Cloud.

### Action Plan:
* Test the Floci project to practice AWS labs more cost-efficiently.
* Practice active communication, teamwork skills, and minimize procrastination in daily tasks.
* Explore deploying applications to AWS using the modern service models shared during the event.
