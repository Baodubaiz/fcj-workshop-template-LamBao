**On this page**, you will find the 12-week worklog outlining the design, implementation, and deployment of the **NovaTech eCommerce** platform. Over the course of 12 weeks, the project progress is divided into five key development phases:

* **Phase 1: Foundation & Contract (Weeks 1-3)**
* **Phase 2: Core Business & MVP (Weeks 4-6)**
* **Phase 3: Advanced Features & Scaling (Weeks 7-9)**
* **Phase 4: DevOps & Multi-Platform CI/CD (Weeks 10-11)**
* **Phase 5: GitOps Implementation & Monitoring (Week 12)**

Here is the weekly breakdown:

**Week 1:** [Project Setup & Skeleton (Spring Boot 11 Layers & Next.js App Router)](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Security Configuration & Basic Authentication (JWT & Spring Security)](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [FE-BE Integration, Middleware Logging & Docker Prep](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Product Variant System (Laptop Configurations & Soft Delete)](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Cart System (Local Storage & DB Sync) & Data Seeding](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Stock Reservation System & Payment Simulation](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Caching Layer with Redis (Category, Session & OTP Cache)](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Search Engine Optimization (PostgreSQL Full-text Search)](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Message Queue (Asynchronous Email Processing) & Voucher System](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Containerization & Orchestration via Docker Compose](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Multi-Platform CI/CD Pipelines (GitHub Actions & GitLab CI/CD)](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Prometheus/Grafana Monitoring & GitOps Deployment](1.12-week12/)
